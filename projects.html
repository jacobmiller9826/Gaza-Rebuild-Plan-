Current Ideas Under Development

  • 1. Trailer Rebuild & Mobility

    My Tucson trailer taught me that a stable home doesn’t have to be built from scratch — it can be reclaimed. Using modular trailer designs, reclaimed wood, solar panels, and local metalwork, we could create affordable, mobile housing units for displaced families worldwide.

  • 2. Recycled Material Marketplaces

    A decentralized platform (blockchain-backed) for buying, trading, and tracking reclaimed building materials. It ensures transparency, fair pricing, and eco-conscious construction while empowering local artisans.

  • 3. Gaza Green Zones

    Converting bombed-out lots into small-scale urban gardens and solar farms. Community-led reforestation projects, using irrigation systems powered by salvaged car batteries and water pumps.

  • 4. The Education Network

    Open workshops and digital tutorials teaching anyone — from Tucson to Gaza — how to rebuild with their hands, not just rely on outside help. Every project becomes both a home and a classroom.
