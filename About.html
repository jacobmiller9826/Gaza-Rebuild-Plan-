About the Initiative

The Gaza Rebuild Plan is a collaborative vision to reconstruct Gaza through sustainability, transparency, and empowerment. Founded on open-source principles, we aim to unite builders, designers, and citizens around the world to support meaningful recovery.

Every element — from building plans to budgets — is open to public contribution. Our mission is to rebuild with integrity and share blueprints globally for free use.
