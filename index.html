Gaza Rebuild Plan

Empowering communities to rebuild sustainably — one block, one family, one future at a time.

Our Mission

The Gaza Rebuild Plan is an open-source, community-driven initiative to restore housing, schools, and infrastructure using sustainable methods and local materials. By combining technology, transparency, and human empathy, we aim to build a stronger and more resilient Gaza — for everyone.

Rebuild Projects

Each project focuses on reconstructing neighborhoods with renewable energy, clean water access, and eco-friendly architecture. Explore open data and real-time progress updates below.

🏠 Housing Renewal

Modular, affordable homes built using local materials and open-source blueprints.

💧 Clean Water Access

Rebuilding pipelines and wells using sustainable engineering and solar power.

📚 Education Spaces

Open-air learning environments and digitally connected classrooms for youth.
